REGULATORY STATEMENT — The Resilient Path Application
=====================================================

Product: "Managing Life with Chronic Pain: The Resilient Path"
Developer: Brewster Wickersham Publications
Bundle ID: com.resilientpath.app
Date: May 12, 2026

CLASSIFICATION: Educational / Informational Wellness Tool

This statement is provided in response to Apple App Store Review Guideline 1.4.1 
regarding applications that provide health-related information.

STATEMENT OF NON-MEDICAL DEVICE STATUS
---------------------------------------

"The Resilient Path" is an educational and informational wellness companion 
application based on the published book "Managing Life with Chronic Pain: 
The Resilient Path." It is designed to complement — not replace — professional 
medical care.

This application:

  1. Does NOT perform any medical device functions as defined by the FDA 
     (21 CFR Part 820) or any international regulatory body.

  2. Does NOT provide medical diagnoses, clinical assessments, or diagnostic 
     conclusions of any kind.

  3. Does NOT recommend, prescribe, or suggest specific medical treatments, 
     medications, or therapeutic interventions.

  4. Does NOT measure, record, or analyze physiological signals or biomarkers 
     for clinical purposes.

  5. Is NOT intended to be used in the diagnosis of disease or other conditions, 
     or in the cure, mitigation, treatment, or prevention of disease.

WHAT THE APPLICATION DOES PROVIDE:

  - A digital companion workbook with guided journaling exercises based on 
    published educational materials about chronic pain self-management.

  - An AI-powered chat feature that provides educational responses based solely 
    on the published book content. This feature explicitly instructs users to 
    consult their healthcare providers and does not provide personalized 
    medical advice.

  - Self-tracking tools (symptom logs, medication lists, doctor visit notes) 
    that allow users to organize and share their own health information with 
    their healthcare providers. These tools do not analyze, interpret, or 
    provide clinical meaning to the data entered.

  - Emergency resource links directing users to professional crisis services.

IN-APP SAFEGUARDS:

  - A mandatory medical disclaimer is displayed on EVERY app launch, explicitly 
    stating the app is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide medical advice.

  - Users must acknowledge the disclaimer before accessing any app content.

  - The AI chat feature includes persistent banners reminding users that 
    responses are educational only, not medical advice.

  - The AI chat system prompt explicitly prohibits the AI from providing 
    diagnoses or prescribing medications.

  - A self-compassion statement encourages users to approach their health 
    journey with patience and to work alongside their medical providers.

REGULATORY CONCLUSION:

Based on the above, "The Resilient Path" does not meet the definition of a 
medical device under FDA regulations, EU MDR 2017/745, or equivalent 
international frameworks. It is an educational wellness tool that explicitly 
directs users to seek professional medical guidance for all health decisions.

No regulatory clearance or approval is required for this application.
